About The Event

The Department of Computer Application in collaboration with Student association STACC of Department of Computer Application, CUSAT is organizing the signature event Resurrection'23 that provides a platform for students to tap their potential and unravel their quiescent talents. Students from various backgrounds and cultures will get an opportunity to interact and appreciate one and other. It will bestow students with an unforgettable learning experience as Resurrection'23 offers a whole gamut of events.

Where

DCA, CUSAT

When

Thursday to Friday
23 - 24 March 2023

  • Shall transport facility be provided by the college?

    No, There will not be transportation facilities/ arrangements from the side of college.

  • Is there a clock room arranged for the participants on the first day of the event?

    Yes, Clock room will be provided for the participants for your baggage.

  • What shall be the remedial measures/first aid provided incase of medical emergencies/injuries?

    Yes,The participants can immediately contact the coordinators.

  • What are the dates of Resurrection'23?

    March 23 and 24. Events will run from about 10 am until 4 pm.

  • How can i participate in Resurrection'23?

    Registration links shall be aired in the portal, 2 weeks prior to the event dates of Resurrection'23
